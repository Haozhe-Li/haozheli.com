About This Website

ChatAI on Haozheli.com (hereinafter referred to as "this website") offers you the opportunity to seamlessly use ChatGPT within mainland China. This website is built upon the open-source project Pandora available on GitHub, with custom modifications and optimizations for deployment on Haozheli.com. We extend our heartfelt gratitude to the creators of the Pandora project for their invaluable support to this endeavor. Please note that this website is not an official service provided by OpenAI and exists purely for entertainment purposes.


Frequently Asked Questions and Answers


Is this free?

Yes.


How do I use this website?

To utilize this website, you need an OpenAI account and should log in using your credentials. If you don't have an account yet, kindly proceed to OpenAI for registration (We don't provide third-party login eg. Google, Apple yet). Rest assured, as a static website, your login information will be securely transmitted to OpenAI for authentication, and this website will neither disclose nor store your account credentials. However, if you still have concerns about security risks, please exercise discretion in using this website.


Encountering login issues?

Given that this website is in the Beta stage, there might be occasional instability or the possibility of sudden closures. If you encounter difficulties during login, please attempt to refresh the page. If the issue persists, feel free to contact me.


Is a VPN required?

Based on current experience, a VPN is not necessary at the moment. However, we cannot rule out the possibility of it being required in the future.


Have more questions?

If you have additional inquiries or require further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to me.
