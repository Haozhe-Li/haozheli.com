ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Haozhe Li, or Howard, and I'm a computer science and music major at the University of Illinois. "A cellist who loves programming" is the most accurate statement that can define me. I have studied cello since I was a child and participated in symphony orchestras for almost 10 years as a principal cellist. After entering college life, I gradually became interested in computer programming and began to learn programming languages such as Python and Java.

I am also a personal video creator, I often upload tutorials on VLOG, cinematic shots and video editing effects on Bilibili and Youtube， and have received millions of views.
