ABOUT ME

Greetings, my name is Haozhe Li, also known as Howard, and I am a student majoring in Computer Science and Music at the University of Illinois. I am accurately described as a "cellist who loves programming", as I have a passion for both classical music and computer technology. I have had a lifelong love affair with the cello, having begun my studies as a child and serving as a principal cellist in symphony orchestras for nearly a decade. During my college years, I developed an interest in computer programming and have since gained proficiency in programming languages such as Python and Java.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I am also a personal video creator. I frequently publish tutorials, vlogs, and showcase my skills in creating cinematic shots and video editing effects on platforms such as Bilibili and YouTube. I have garnered significant viewership, with millions of views on my content.
