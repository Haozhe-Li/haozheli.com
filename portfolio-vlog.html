I am a person who is passionate about travel, so I started shooting travel videos and VLOGs to document my journeys since high school. Compared to shooting on set, travel videos come with more uncertainties, which is why they do not involve storyboard designs. Therefore, I do my best to capture footage using cameras, drones, and other equipment during the pre-production phase. In post-production, I am also skilled in using professional editing software such as DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Final Cut Pro for video editing, color grading, and special effects production.
